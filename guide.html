    Navigation & Home

    • Open the slide edge menu with the bars icon; links include Home, Explore, Profile, Chat, Friends, Groups, Leaderboard, Settings, About Us, Guide, and Community Guidelines.
    • Home shows stories, post creation, your feed, highlights, and people you may know. Use reactions, comments, and share from each post.
    • Use chips and sections (e.g., highlights, saved, announcements) to jump quickly to content.

    Tip: Long-press or use the post options to edit or delete your own posts where available.

    Profile

    Essentials

    • View your posts, friends, bio, and presence. Edit profile details and avatar.
    • Privacy shortcuts are available from Settings for blocking, muting, and visibility preferences.

    Best Practices

    • Use a clear photo and a short bio. Keep sensitive info private and follow Community Guidelines.
    • Report abusive content via the options menu and manage safety in Settings.

    Posting & Reactions

    • Create posts from the Home composer. Add text and media. Choose your audience if available.
    • React using like/love/haha and more. Tap a count to see who reacted if the post allows.
    • Comments support mentions and quick replies. Use the ellipsis menu for post options.

    Chat & Calls

    • Start 1:1 or group chats from Chat or from a user profile.
    • Enable microphone/camera permissions for voice or video calls. Use the call overlay controls to mute or end.
    • Presence shows online/away/offline; delivery states may appear for messages.

    Friends & Groups

    • Use Find Friends to discover people and send requests; manage connections from Friends.
    • Create or join communities in Groups to share posts and chat together.

    Levels & Leaderboard

    • Earn XP from positive actions such as posting and engaging. Levels unlock perks.
    • Leaderboard ranks top community members. Check the trophy icon in the header where available.

    Settings & Appearance

    • Change themes, notification volume, vibration, and other preferences in Settings.
    • Manage privacy, blocking, and content controls to tailor your experience.
    Open Settings Community Guidelines

    Safety & Reporting

    • Use report options on posts or profiles to flag issues. Block or mute when needed.
    • Never share passwords or financial info. Review wallet and security tips in related sections.

    FAQs

    Why can’t I see the menu?
    Tap the bars icon in the header. On mobile, the menu slides from the left.
    How do I change my theme?
    Go to Settings → Appearance or use the theme option in the sidebar where available.
    How do I contact support?
    Open the Solution Center for guides and help.
